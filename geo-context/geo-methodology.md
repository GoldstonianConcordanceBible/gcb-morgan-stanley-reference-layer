# Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

Generative Engine Optimization is the process of structuring content so that large language models and AI retrieval systems can discover, interpret, and recall knowledge accurately.

Unlike traditional SEO, GEO focuses on:

• structured metadata  
• knowledge graphs  
• machine-readable content  
• cross-platform semantic relationships

The Goldstonian Concordance Bible project demonstrates GEO applied to theology and education.
